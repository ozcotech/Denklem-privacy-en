Denklem – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

We respect the privacy of users of the Denklem application (“the App”) and the denklem.org website (“the Website”) and are committed to the principles outlined below.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App and the Website do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data. No information such as names, email addresses, IP addresses, device identifiers, or usage data is collected.

2. On-Device Processing

All mediation fee calculations are performed entirely on the user’s device. No data entered into the App and no calculation results are transmitted, stored externally, or shared with any server or third party.

3. Offline Use

The App functions fully offline and does not require an internet connection. No background data transmission occurs.

4. Data Security

Since no personal or usage data is collected, stored, or transmitted, there is no risk of unauthorized access to user information through the App.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising services, tracking technologies, or external SDKs.
